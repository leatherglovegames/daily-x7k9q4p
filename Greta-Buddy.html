01 Status

Shipped + Flashed v19

Arcade-cabinet life-sim of Dad running on a physical ESP32 device in a 3D-printed retro-TV case. In Greta's hands since June 2026. Retired under the Finished Projects Convention after post-ship patches C47 through C50.

02 What It Is

A pixel-art life-sim where 10-year-old Greta raises a tiny 8-bit version of her dad. Feed him, play with him, put him to work, check his mail. An economy with coins, a daily-rotating shop, buy-once unlockable jobs and foods, and stat bars for FOOD, FUN, WORK, and MAIL. Idle too long and a screensaver kicks in. Tap the dog (Ripley) to pet him. On November 25th, the buddy knows it's Greta's birthday.

A physical object that sits on Greta's desk at Mom's house and reminds her that Dad is always thinking about her.
  • Life-sim economy — coins, shop progression, daily-rotating store, stat management
  • Table clock — NTP-synced real time display, build-time anchor when offline
  • Idle screensaver — kicks in after inactivity, doubles as the message display
  • Picture Frame mode — Dad sends a Photo of the Day that lives on the screensaver for 24 hours
  • Dad's status line — a short text blurb from Dad, shown on screen with a 24h TTL
  • Countdown widget — Dad sets a countdown to the next visit or event
  • Birthday moment — special behavior on November 25th
  • Ripley tap-to-pet — tap the family dog for a reaction
  • Dual-home WiFi — remembers the last 2 networks plus 2 built-in defaults

04 Message System

Adam sends love-notes, photos, status updates, and countdowns from an HTML page hosted on Cloudflare. Messages ride through a shared Cloudflare Worker (also used by Melissa-Buddy) and land on the device's screensaver.

FeatureHow It Works
Love notes240-char messages composed in browser, delivered to device inbox, paged letter view with tap-to-read-more
Photo of the Day24h auto-expiring photo, auto-resized in browser before upload
Status lineShort text blurb (14 chars), 24h TTL on device
CountdownLabel + target date, device counts down days remaining
DeliveryCloudflare Worker + KV store, recipient-isolated routing, Discord webhook relay
Offline resilienceQueued messages survive WiFi drops; "SAVED! WILL SEND WHEN WIFI'S BACK"
Shared Infrastructure

The Cloudflare Worker is shared with Melissa-Buddy. Greta routes use a recipient tag; retiring Greta did NOT touch the worker. Melissa still depends on it.

06 Key Architecture Decisions
  • No framebuffer — all rendering is immediate-mode direct draw to the ILI9341. Cross-fades can only blend toward a solid color (navy/cream); smooth transitions use instant opaque swaps instead.
  • Core 0 networking — net polls (mail, photo, status, countdown, weather) run on core 0, spaced so only one TLS handshake stacks per iteration to guard the no-PSRAM heap.
  • NVS migration — legacy 208-byte NVS rings auto-migrate to the new InboxMsg layout (text[243] + per-note read flag). Saved notes survive the update.
  • Hardware pivot — originally started on ESP32-C3 + ST7789, pivoted 2026-05-10 to share the CYD platform with Melissa-Buddy.
