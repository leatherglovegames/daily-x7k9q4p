A pixel-art dating-show game built for Adam's fiancee Melissa, running on the same ESP32 CYD hardware as the Greta Buddy. Full game loop plays end-to-end. Bidirectional messaging live via Cloudflare Worker. Currently in maintenance mode with Q&A content walkthrough paused at Q13.

02 The Game

Three multiverse pixel-art Adams stand behind curtains, Dating Game style (1965). Melissa interviews them through a Q&A round, picks her favorite, watches a curtain reveal, then plays through a five-choice date. Score high enough and she takes him home with a randomized reveal from the END tile pool. Tone: adult, flirty, romantic. Framing: fun game, not competitive.

INTRO → Q&A × 3 → PICK → REVEAL → DATE × 5 → OUTCOME → REPLAY
  • 17 bachelor archetypes — biker, chef, cowboy, director, lawyer, surfer, tux, wrestler, renfest, firefighter, rockstar, doctor, pirate, astronaut, detective, lumberjack, vampire count
  • 60-question Q&A bank — 8 answers each, Mr. Show humor pass, curated voice
  • Scoring — love +1, fine +0.5, hate -0.5 + early end. Threshold: 3+ hearts to take him home
  • Hidden mechanics — SUPER answers (~10% stealth roll), gay-reveal (negative score + coin flip), vampire secret END (~25% override)
  • 52 pixel-art tiles — Hyper Light Drifter art direction, RGB565 native at 100x100
  • Audio jingles — ~10 cues via the on-board speaker
